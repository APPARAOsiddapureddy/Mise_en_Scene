Mise-en-Scene Events

Mise-en-scène refers to the arrangement of visual elements in a film or theatrical production, including sets, props, costumes, lighting, and actors' movements within the frame. It's a fundamental aspect of visual storytelling, influencing the mood, atmosphere, and themes of a work.

In this club , popularly known as the film club we have done many movie marathons Where questions on cinema will be asked to the participants and they will be guessing the right answers.Participants will be forming groups and which ever group have the highest score they will be receiving prize money if nearly 2k.And also from this club students have performed some skits in many events Like during uthkrista and freshers and abhisarga.

Abhisarga

This is the biggest event celebrated in the college. This event is celebrated for 3 days, where many students participate in different activities like dance, singing,stage performances etc And also movie marathons will be conducted with a prize money during this event.


Students will be communicating with the outside world to collect the funds for the event which helps them to build good relationships with the companies and also good communication skills. There are many opportunities for the students in this event. Many great personalities will be visiting our college to motivate and inspire the student.



Uthkrista

Generally this event takes place during the month of November. Uthkrishta" is a Sanskrit term that translates to "excellence" or "superiority" in English.


During this event many dance performances, singing, instrumental music, beat boxing,stage performances, and prize distribution for the students who are the part of many sports activities and movie marathons.
