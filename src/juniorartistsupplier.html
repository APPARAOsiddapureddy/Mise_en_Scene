Junior Artist Supplier

As a Junior Artist Supplier in the film industry, your role primarily involves providing artistic resources and services to support the production of films, TV shows, commercials, and other visual media projects.

This involves liaising with vendors, negotiating contracts, and ensuring timely delivery of materials within budgetary constraints. You'll collaborate closely with production teams to understand their artistic requirements, providing support in areas such as props, costumes, sets, and other visual elements. Your responsibilities also include inventory management, budgeting, problem-solving, and compliance with industry regulations and safety standards.

By effectively coordinating with various departments and staying updated on industry trends, you play a vital role in bringing the artistic vision of each project to fruition.
